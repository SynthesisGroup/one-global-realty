About Us

Who We Are

Manila Realty is a real-estate company that provides listings to its customers through an online platform. The Company aims to create effortless transactions wherein buyers are assured reliable and quality service.

Based in the Philippines, the platform caters customers, looking for properties within Metro Manila.
